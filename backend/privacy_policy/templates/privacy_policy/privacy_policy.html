Privacy Policy

What we collect

For Applicants

If you have an account, we only collect your email address and whatever information you choose to put on your profiles.

If you do not have an account, we collect nothing.

We do not use cookies or trackers, either first-party or third-party, to collect your activities and metadata generated while visiting the site.

For Employers

We collect your email address, business address, company name, and whatever other information you choose to put in your profile.

We also obviously collect and store your job posts but do not collect any job application metadata.

When doing document verification, we collect your uploaded documents but will delete them immediately when verification is complete.

We do not use cookies or trackers, either first-party or third-party, to collect your activities and metadata generated while visiting the site.

How we collect it

For Applicants

Your email and profile information are collected at creation time by storing what you input for those fields.

For Employers

Your email and profile information are collected at creation time by storing what you input for those fields.

Your documents are stored for verification when you upload them.

Why we collect it

For Applicants

Your email is only used to exchange with the employer's email that you have applied for once they have chosen to show interest in your profile.

Your profile information is only given to the employer after you apply for a job.

We do not sell, identify or process your personal information for profit or analytical purposes in any way.

For Employers

Your email is only used to exchange with an applicant's email after you express intrest in an applicant's profile after they have applied to one of your job postings.

Your profile information is only used to display information about your company to applicants.

Your documents are only used to verify the information you enter in your profile to prevent cases of fraud.

We do not sell, identify or process your personal information or information about the company for profit or analytical purposes in any way.

Suggestions for applicants using the site

For maximum privacy, consider using an anonymous email or email masking service, so that anyone getting access to your email cannot trace your identity backwards.

It is possible and recommended to use an alias to create an applicant file.

To ensure maximum privacy, it is also recommended to not include any personal identifier like driverâ€™s license number, postal code or phone number in any applicant profile field.

If you need to include third-party sites that include your work, it would be best to configure that site so that you are not traceable from there either.

Semi-identifiers like the city you are currently living in, the universities you went to, or the companies you worked for previously might be necessary for a decent knowledge about the applicant and for a HR personnel to be interested in you.

You can trust your profile with us: we have an encryption program that encrypts your profiles before then are stored, and we do not simply retrieve your profile, decrypt it, and start reading it either.

You have unlimited access to your stored profile content, and you can view, modify and delete your profile content at any time.

Your profile information is immediately wiped from our database upon its deletion. We do not retain your profiles for an extended amount of time.
